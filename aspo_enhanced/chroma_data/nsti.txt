NSTI Bengaluru – Complete Overview

National Skill Training Institute Bengaluru (NSTI Bengaluru) is a premier Government of India training institute under the Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship and Directorate General of Training (DGT). It is located at Yeswanthpur, Bengaluru and focuses on instructor training, advanced vocational training, skill development programs, and industry-oriented technical education.

History

NSTI Bengaluru was established in 1968 with assistance from the State of Baden-Württemberg, Germany. It was originally created to upgrade technical and supervisory skills and later became one of India's leading vocational training institutes. It has been recognized as a Centre of Excellence for Supervisory Training and Development.
